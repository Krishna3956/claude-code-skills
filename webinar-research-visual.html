Part 3

Key Insights & Unsolved Problems

What matters most — and what nobody has figured out yet.

🧠 What Actually Drives Results

1

Overlay Engagement

Polls ON video (not sidebar) = ~3x engagement. Only Contrast does this. Everyone else uses sidebars.

2

Shorter Formats

30-min webinars get 2x completion rate vs 60-min. The market hasn't adapted — most platforms still default to 60 min.

3

First 60 Seconds

If the opening is boring, 30% leave immediately. Auto-intros with countdown timers and branded videos solve this.

4

Interaction Every 5-7 Min

Poll, CTA, Q&A prompt, or reaction moment. Regular participation triggers commit attendees to staying.

5

Post-Event = The Real Value

Live event is 1 hour. Content from it should drive pipeline for weeks. Make the content engine the product.

6

Stability > Features

Host's #1 fear: "What if it breaks live?" Reliability reduces stress more than any feature ever will.

🔴 Major Unsolved Problems

Setup is still 15+ minutes

No platform delivers true "60-second webinar setup." The fastest (Contrast) takes ~5 min. Room for 10x improvement.

AI is shallow everywhere

AI only used post-event for clips. Nobody uses AI for creation, live moderation, real-time insights, or prescriptive analytics.

Attendees are passive

45 min lecture + 15 min Q&A. No networking (except Hopin). No personalization. No reason to stay the full time.

Analytics = data dumps

"Here's what happened." Never "Here's what to do differently." No platform provides prescriptive, actionable insights.

Mobile is broken

All platforms are desktop-first. No one has a good mobile host OR attendee experience. Uncontested space.

No webinar series orchestration

Running a 6-part series = managing 6 separate events. No platform offers program-level orchestration.

🎙️ Host Journey

💬

1. Create

60 seconds

Type one sentence: "Product launch webinar for our new AI feature." AI generates title, description, agenda, branded registration page, email sequence, and engagement plan.

✨ Fully designed webinar page from a single prompt — before you even think about promotion.
📣

2. Promote

Zero manual work

Reg page auto-live. One-click share to LinkedIn/X/email with AI-written copy. Smart embed widget (countdown → player → replay). AI A/B tests headlines.

✨ Share button pre-fills perfectly written social posts. Just click publish.
🎯

3. Prepare

Confidence before live

Speakers get personalized prep pages (not generic emails) with their talking points and tech check. AI rehearsal feedback: "Your intro is 3 min — shorten to 90 sec." Green room 15 min before.

✨ AI: "All speakers connected. Audio levels good. You're ready."
🔴

4. Go Live

Produced show, zero effort

Auto countdown → branded intro → host appears. AI manages layouts (auto-switches based on content). Engagement overlays fire at pre-planned optimal moments. AI groups Q&A, moderates chat, alerts on attention drops.

✨ AI nudge: "Engagement peaking — perfect time for your CTA." Polls appear magically.
📦

5. Repurpose

1 webinar = 1 month of content

Within minutes: same URL becomes chaptered replay. AI generates 12 branded clips, blog post draft, 8 social posts, and personalized follow-up emails based on attendee behavior.

✨ Go to lunch, come back to a full content calendar and personalized follow-ups — ready to publish.
📊

6. Analyze

Prescriptive, not descriptive

AI: "Your poll at min 7 spiked engagement 40%. Retention improved 12% — shorter intro helped. These 15 attendees are ready for sales outreach. Next topic should be [X] based on Q&A data."

✨ AI pre-creates your next webinar based on audience demand signals.

Opinionated Conclusions

10 Things You Need to Know

The strategic takeaways that should inform every product decision.

01

Contrast is the platform to beat

They own "modern webinars for SaaS." But their AI is shallow and they have zero networking or automation.

02

Welcome is vulnerable

Expensive, sales-gated, uncertain future. Their enterprise customers may be open to alternatives with better value + AI.

03

AI is the only real moat

Everyone has reg pages, chat, email. AI across the full lifecycle is what nobody has. That's the gap.

04

"One sentence" is your headline

Contrast says "2 clicks." You say "One sentence." 60-second AI setup is your marketing hook.

05

Don't build for conferences

Hopin owns large events. Win by being the best at recurring marketing webinars (50-500 attendees).

06

Charge per event, not per registrant

Every competitor punishes success with per-registrant pricing. Event-based pricing is a positioning advantage.

07

Post-event is where the money is

The live event is 1 hour. Content should drive pipeline for weeks. Make repurposing so good it alone justifies the platform.

08

Mobile-native is uncontested

No platform has a good mobile host experience. "Host from your phone" is a marketing wedge nobody else can claim.

09

Speed networking is underrated

Hopin proved 1:1 random pairing works. Nobody else has copied it. Steal it for your webinar platform.

10

Your real competitor is inaction

Many marketers avoid webinars because setup is painful. Make it effortless and you expand the market, not just steal share.
