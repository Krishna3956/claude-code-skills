Journey Verdicts

The emotional arc, time-to-value, and strategic takeaway for each platform.

⚡ Contrast

"Refreshingly simple" → "Looks professional" → "Content for the week"

~5m
Time to Value
Low
Host Stress
High
Attendee UX
  • Best signup-to-first-webinar flow in the category
  • Overlay engagement is genuinely unique — nobody else does it
  • Repurpose AI extends value beyond the live event
  • Gap: No automation, no networking, no AI during creation or live
Win by being faster (AI setup) + smarter (AI during live) + more social (networking)

🎬 Welcome

"This feels premium" → "Setup is complex" → "Content output is amazing"

Days
Time to Value
High
Host Stress
High
Attendee UX
  • Best production quality in the category (Welcome Studio)
  • Sales-gated = huge time-to-value problem. Days before first webinar.
  • $625/mo kills adoption. No PLG motion at all.
  • Gap: Price, self-serve, automation, networking, uncertain future
Their customers want production quality but can't justify $625/mo. Offer it at $99/mo with AI.

🔄 Livestorm

"Easy to start" → "Automation is great" → "Where's the AI?"

~10m
Time to Value
Low
Host Stress
Medium
Attendee UX
  • Best recurring/evergreen webinar automation
  • 15-20% higher attendance vs Zoom (no download)
  • "Active Contact" pricing trap — costs scale unpredictably
  • Gap: No AI, no repurposing, no production quality, bland live UX
Steal their evergreen automation. Beat them with AI + production quality + better pricing.

"Affordable" → "Great for sales" → "Limited for scale"

~10m

Attendee UX

  • Attendee-on-stage = great for interactive sessions
  • Gap: No AI, no content repurposing, limited CRM, dated UI
Not a direct competitor if you target mid-market. Steal their urgency timers idea.
